# Work From Home Policy

**Category:** ATTENDANCE  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026  
**Policy Owner:** People Operations

---

## 1. Overview

NovaWorks Technologies supports a hybrid work model. Employees may work from home (WFH) subject to the guidelines in this policy.

## 2. WFH Eligibility

- All permanent full-time employees who have completed their probation period (90 days) are eligible for WFH.
- Contract and part-time employees may work remotely based on project requirements and manager approval.
- Employees on probation must work from office unless an exception is granted by HR.

## 3. WFH Entitlement

- Employees are entitled to a maximum of **2 WFH days per week** under the standard hybrid model.
- The specific WFH days must be agreed upon with the direct manager at the start of each month.
- WFH is not permitted on Mondays and Fridays unless approved by the department head due to team alignment needs.

## 4. WFH Application Process

1. Inform your manager at least 24 hours in advance via CB Nest or email.
2. Manager approval is required before logging WFH attendance.
3. Ad hoc WFH requests on the same day require messaging the manager directly and updating the attendance system.

## 5. Responsibilities While Working From Home

- Employees must be reachable on Slack and email during core working hours (10 AM – 6 PM IST).
- Attend all scheduled meetings via video call with camera on.
- Maintain regular productivity and deliverable timelines.
- Protect company data: do not access internal systems from public or unsecured Wi-Fi without a VPN.

## 6. Equipment and Connectivity

- Employees are responsible for maintaining a stable internet connection for WFH days.
- Company-issued laptops must be used for all work from home activities.
- Peripherals (headsets, webcams) may be requested via the IT Helpdesk if needed.

## 7. Full Remote Work (Extended WFH)

- Extended full-remote arrangements (more than 1 month) require written approval from the department head and HR Director.
- Full remote work is reviewed quarterly and may be revoked based on business needs.

## 8. Monitoring and Compliance

- WFH usage is tracked in the CB Nest attendance system.
- Managers review WFH patterns quarterly. Misuse may result in WFH privileges being revoked.
- Logging WFH attendance without manager approval is a policy violation.
