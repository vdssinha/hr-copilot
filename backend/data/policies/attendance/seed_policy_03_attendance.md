# Attendance Policy

**Category:** ATTENDANCE  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026  
**Policy Owner:** People Operations

---

## 1. Working Hours

- Standard working hours at NovaWorks are **9 hours per day**, including a 1-hour lunch break.
- Core hours during which all employees must be available are **10:00 AM to 5:00 PM IST**.
- Flexible start times are permitted between 8:30 AM and 10:30 AM, as long as the 9-hour total is met.

## 2. Late Arrival

- Arriving more than 15 minutes after the agreed start time is considered a late arrival.
- 3 late arrivals in a calendar month result in a half-day deduction from the employee's casual leave balance.
- If casual leave is exhausted, the deduction is taken from annual leave. If annual leave is also exhausted, it is treated as Loss of Pay (LOP).
- Managers must be informed immediately if an employee expects to be late.

## 3. Early Departure

- Leaving more than 30 minutes before the standard end time without manager approval is considered early departure.
- Early departure is treated similarly to late arrival for deduction purposes.

## 4. Attendance Regularization

- Employees who forget to mark attendance or face system issues may regularize their attendance within **3 working days** through the CB Nest portal.
- Regularization requests exceeding 3 days require manager and HR approval.
- Excessive regularization requests (more than 5 per quarter) will be flagged to the department head.

## 5. Absence Without Information (AWOL)

- Failure to report to work or notify the manager for 2 consecutive working days without approved leave is considered Absence Without Leave (AWOL).
- AWOL may result in disciplinary action, including salary deduction and formal warning.
- HR will attempt to contact the employee on day 1 of unexplained absence.

## 6. Overtime

- Overtime is not a standard expectation and should not be routine.
- Where required for project delivery, overtime must be pre-approved by the department head.
- Overtime compensation or compensatory off is at the discretion of the department head and HR.

## 7. Public Holidays and Weekend Work

- Employees working on weekends or public holidays at company request are eligible for compensatory off within 30 days.
- Voluntary weekend or holiday work does not automatically entitle employees to compensatory off.
