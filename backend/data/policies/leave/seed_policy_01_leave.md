# Leave Policy

**Category:** LEAVE  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026  
**Policy Owner:** People Operations

---

## 1. Leave Entitlement

All full-time employees at NovaWorks Technologies are entitled to the following leaves per calendar year:

| Leave Type | Days Per Year |
|------------|---------------|
| Casual Leave (CL) | 12 days |
| Sick Leave (SL) | 12 days |
| Annual/Earned Leave (AL) | 15 days |
| Unpaid Leave | As approved |

Part-time and contract employees are entitled to pro-rated leave based on their working hours.

## 2. Sick Leave

- Employees may take up to 12 sick leave days per year.
- Sick leave is intended for personal illness or medical appointments.
- For sick leave exceeding 3 consecutive days, a medical certificate from a registered medical practitioner is required.
- Sick leave cannot be carried forward to the next calendar year.
- Sick leave cannot be encashed.

## 3. Casual Leave

- Employees may take up to 12 casual leave days per year for personal reasons, family obligations, or short-notice needs.
- Casual leave should be applied at least 1 day in advance wherever possible.
- A maximum of 3 consecutive casual leave days may be taken at once. For longer periods, annual leave should be used.
- Unused casual leave lapses at the end of the calendar year and cannot be carried forward or encashed.

## 4. Annual / Earned Leave

- Employees accrue 15 days of annual leave per year.
- Annual leave must be applied at least 7 days in advance and is subject to manager approval.
- A maximum of 10 days of annual leave may be carried forward to the following year.
- Annual leave can be encashed up to a maximum of 5 days per year, subject to business requirements and HR approval.
- New employees are eligible for annual leave after completing 6 months of service.

## 5. Half-Day Leave

- Employees may apply for half-day leave for either the morning (first half) or afternoon (second half) session.
- Half-day leave deducts 0.5 days from the applicable leave balance.
- Half-day leave must be applied at least 4 hours in advance.

## 6. Leave Application Process

1. Log in to the CB Nest HRMS portal or use the HR Copilot.
2. Navigate to Leave Management → Apply for Leave.
3. Select leave type, date range, and provide a reason.
4. Submit for manager approval.
5. You will receive an email notification once your manager approves or rejects the request.

## 7. Leave Without Pay (Unpaid Leave)

- Employees who exhaust all paid leave balances may apply for unpaid leave.
- Unpaid leave requires prior written approval from the department manager and HR.
- Unpaid leave affects monthly payroll; salary is deducted proportionally.

## 8. Leave Restrictions

- Leave cannot be taken during probation period (first 90 days) without prior HR approval.
- Leave during project critical phases is subject to manager discretion.
- Unapproved absence is treated as Loss of Pay (LOP).

## 9. Holidays

- NovaWorks observes all national public holidays as declared by the government.
- A list of company holidays is published at the start of each calendar year.
- Employees working on a public holiday are eligible for a compensatory off to be availed within 30 days.
