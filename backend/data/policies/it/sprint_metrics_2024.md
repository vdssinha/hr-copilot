## Executive Summary

FinSolve Technologies' Engineering team executed 26 bi-weekly sprints throughout 2024, delivering significant feature releases and maintaining system stability. The team demonstrated consistent velocity improvement, growing from an average of 165 story points in Q1 to 212 story points in Q4 (28% growth). Technical debt initiatives maintained code health at 85% test coverage, while deployment frequency reached 104 times across the year.

**Key Metrics:**
- **Total Story Points Delivered:** 4,982
- **Completion Rate:** 94.2%
- **Average Velocity:** 192 story points per sprint
- **Bugs Fixed:** 287
- **Features Shipped:** 156
- **Deployments:** 104 (2 per week average)
- **Team Growth:** 57 → 63 engineers (11% growth)

## Retrospective Insights

### What Went Well
1. **Consistent Velocity Growth:** 32.7% improvement Q1→Q4 through better estimation and process optimization
2. **Code Quality Improvements:** Test coverage increased from 82% to 85%, bug escape rates dropped 57%
3. **Deployment Stability:** 99.3% success rate maintained with only 1 rollback in 52 production deployments
4. **Team Collaboration:** Code review times halved from 8.2 to 4.6 hours, indicating better knowledge sharing
5. **Technical Excellence:** Completion rate improved from 91.9% to 99.1%

### Areas for Improvement (2025 Goals)
1. **Code Duplication:** Reduce from 6.9% to < 5% through refactoring initiatives
2. **Deployment Speed:** Target < 40 min avg deployment time (current: 39 min)
3. **Feature Time-to-Production:** Reduce average from 61.6 to 45 days through process optimization
4. **Team Growth:** Complete hiring of 6 open positions to reach 69 engineers
5. **Technical Debt:** Increase allocation to 25% to address accumulated complexity
