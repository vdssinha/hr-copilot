# Employee Benefits Policy

**Category:** BENEFITS  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026  
**Policy Owner:** People Operations & Finance

---

## 1. Health Insurance

- All permanent full-time employees are covered under the company's group health insurance from Day 1.
- Coverage extends to the employee, their spouse, and up to 2 dependent children.
- The base sum insured is INR 5,00,000 per family per year.
- Employees may opt for top-up coverage at their own expense through the designated insurance partner.
- Parents and parents-in-law may be added as dependents at an additional premium borne by the employee.

## 2. Provident Fund (PF)

- NovaWorks contributes 12% of the basic salary to the Employee Provident Fund (EPF) as mandated by law.
- Employee contribution is also 12% of basic salary, deducted from monthly salary.
- PF statements are accessible via the EPFO portal.

## 3. Gratuity

- Employees who complete 5 or more years of continuous service are eligible for gratuity as per the Payment of Gratuity Act.
- Gratuity is paid at separation or retirement.

## 4. Meal and Food Benefits

- Employees receive a monthly meal allowance of INR 2,600 (tax-exempt under applicable rules).
- A subsidized cafeteria is available at the Bengaluru headquarters.

## 5. Learning and Development

- Each employee is entitled to an annual L&D budget of INR 25,000 for approved courses, certifications, or conferences.
- Reimbursement requires pre-approval from the manager and submission of receipts to Finance.
- Certifications paid by the company require the employee to remain with the company for at least 12 months post-certification.

## 6. Mobile and Internet Reimbursement

- Employees in roles requiring field work or frequent client interaction are eligible for a monthly mobile reimbursement of up to INR 1,000.
- Employees working from home are eligible for an internet reimbursement of up to INR 1,200 per month upon submission of bills.

## 7. Maternity and Paternity Leave

- Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave as per the Maternity Benefit Act.
- Paternity leave of 10 working days is available for male employees upon the birth or adoption of a child.
- Maternity and paternity leave should be applied at least 2 months in advance wherever possible.

## 8. Employee Referral Program

- Employees who refer candidates who are hired and complete 90 days of service are eligible for a referral bonus.
- Referral bonus amounts vary by role level and are published in the annual HR update.
- Finance, HR, and Senior Leadership roles are excluded from the referral bonus program.

## 9. Annual Health Check-Up

- All employees are entitled to one free annual health check-up per year at designated diagnostic centers.
- The health check-up package covers basic blood panels, vision, dental, and general physician consultation.
- Appointments must be booked through the HR portal and cannot be claimed as a cash reimbursement.
