# Code of Conduct Policy

**Category:** CODE_OF_CONDUCT  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026  
**Policy Owner:** People Operations & Legal

---

## 1. Purpose

This Code of Conduct establishes the standards of behavior expected of all employees, contractors, and partners at NovaWorks Technologies. Our goal is to maintain a respectful, inclusive, and professional workplace.

## 2. Professional Conduct

- Treat all colleagues, clients, and partners with respect and professionalism.
- Communicate constructively and avoid personal attacks, harassment, or discrimination.
- Represent NovaWorks positively in all internal and external interactions.

## 3. Confidentiality

- Employees must protect confidential company information including product roadmaps, financial data, client information, and personnel data.
- Do not share confidential information with unauthorized parties, including family members and former colleagues.
- Confidentiality obligations continue after employment ends.

## 4. Conflicts of Interest

- Disclose any potential conflict of interest to HR and your manager.
- Do not engage in outside employment or business that competes with NovaWorks without written approval.
- Do not accept gifts or benefits from vendors or clients exceeding a value of INR 2,000 without HR approval.

## 5. Use of Company Resources

- Company equipment (laptops, phones, accounts) must be used primarily for work purposes.
- Reasonable personal use is permitted provided it does not interfere with productivity or violate other policies.
- Do not install unauthorized software on company devices.
- Do not use company resources for personal business activities.

## 6. Social Media

- Do not post confidential company information, client details, or unreleased product information on any social media platform.
- Personal social media posts must clearly distinguish personal views from company positions.
- Criticism of clients, colleagues, or the company on public platforms is prohibited.

## 7. Workplace Harassment

- NovaWorks has zero tolerance for harassment of any kind, including verbal, physical, or digital.
- Sexual harassment is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate disciplinary action.
- Employees experiencing or witnessing harassment should report it to HR immediately.

## 8. Disciplinary Action

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:
- Verbal or written warning
- Performance improvement plan
- Suspension without pay
- Termination of employment

The severity of action depends on the nature and frequency of the violation.

## 9. Reporting Violations

- Report violations confidentially to hr@novaworks.in or to your HR Business Partner.
- Retaliation against employees who report violations in good faith is itself a violation of this policy.
